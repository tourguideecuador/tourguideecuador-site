---
question: "What's included in a Galápagos cruise?"
category: "cruises"
order: 3
---

Typically: your cabin, **all meals**, **daily guided excursions** with a certified naturalist, snorkelling gear and transfers between the airport and the boat. Not usually included: international/inter-island flights, the National Park entrance fee, alcoholic drinks, wetsuit rental and tips. Each quote spells out exactly what's covered.
