question: "Do I need a cruise, or is island hopping enough?"
category: "cruises"
order: 2

Both are wonderful — it comes down to what you want. A **cruise** sails overnight to reach remote, uninhabited islands and the best wildlife sites, with a naturalist guiding every excursion. **Island hopping** is more flexible and budget-friendly, based in hotels with day trips from the inhabited islands. We'll happily match you to the right one.
