## WHY CHOOSE US

When you say that you love a place, that means that you have really experienced it.

It’s not only about visiting the old town, it’s also about the secret room behind the stone wall.

It’s not only about walking through the andean market, it’s about having the opportunity to bargain and interact with the local people.

It’s not only about the picture on your instagram next to the Quilotoa crater lake, it’s about the history behind it.

In all these years, we have had the opportunity to meet our country, our people, our richness, and it’s because of that deep love towards our culture, that we wish to show you, what it really means to discover Ecuador.

Give us the opportunity to take you to an authentic and magical place.

## TOUR GUIDE ECUADOR

Tour Guide Ecuador started 5 years ago as a mere dream, like most good ideas in human history.

Our founder is Andres, who has about 20 years of experience as a tour guide. He decided to take the risk and bring this agency to live - moved by a big conviction: the necessity to show people all around the globe, what it really means to be an Ecuadorian. Thereby he never forgets that every passenger is unique and deserves his own personal experience.

This dream finally became reality, became us: Tour Guide Ecuador and our team are working every day in order to give the best possible service to all our passengers.
