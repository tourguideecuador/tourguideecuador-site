## ISLAND HOPPING

### **THE BEST WAY IN GALAPAGOS TOURS**

**IF YOU PREFER A HOTEL ON THE ISLANDS**

Explore Santa Cruz, San Cristobal, Isabela Islands, and more returning to the comfort of a hotel each evening. Snorkel with rays, sea lions, and iguanas, and experience the breathtaking beauty of the Galapagos Islands from the top of the Sierra Negra volcano. An excellent option for those looking for a more active Galapagos Islands tour without spending days at sea

Our Galapagos Islands hopping tour is a great alternative to live-aboard cruises, you get to explore the archipelagos wildlife and natural wonders by day, you sleep in our hotel by night. This allows non-seagoing travelers to enjoy the highlights of the Galapagos Islands without compromising their comfort and need to be on firm land.

Our base program offers fixed departures and is made up of 6 days visiting the unique wonders of the Galapagos Islands wildlife.

### Itinerary 4D/3N - Quick view of Galapagos

DAY 1: Giant Tortoises
After 2 hours of flight from Quito, you will meet tour local represent on Baltra island then you are going to cross the Itabaca channel to get our transport and drive to Highland area in Santa Cruz island, where you will have the opportunity to interact with wild giant tortoise lunch in a local restaurant included, time to go to your hotel for rest and explore by yourself the nightlife of Puerto Ayora.

DAY 2: Around Puerto Ayora
Today you will meet some of the most representatives places around Puerto Ayora such as Franklin Bay and Estrada Point, which are famous because of the possibility to find sea lions, marine tortoises, and blue-footed boobies; also you will have incredible opportunities for practice snorkeling and enjoy the beach. Box lunch included.

DAY 3: Full Day San Cristobal.

Early wake up to catch your speed boat to San Cristobal Island, one of the most important islands in the archipelago, because only here and in Floreana, is possible to find freshwater. Here you will have the opportunity to walk around the same places that Charles Darwin visited some years ago. Lunch in a local restaurant included, back to Santa Cruz by speed boat in the afternoon.

DAY 4: Gemelos – Transfer out
Drive back to Baltra on the way we stop in Gemelos volcano to see this big crater of the Gemelos volcano (twins). Come back to the airport.
