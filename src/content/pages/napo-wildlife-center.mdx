### Itinerary 9D/8N

**DAY 1**

Once you arrive to the airport in San Cristobal, pay the Galapagos National Park entrance fee and claim your luggage. We will receive you just outside and help you to your hotel. After check-in, a delicious lunch awaits you in a local restaurant. Stroll along the boardwalk and get to know the town, then enjoy a different angle, a panga ride in the bay.

**DAY 2**

Today’s feature is one of the best snorkeling sites in the Galapagos Islands, a day tour to Kicker Rock! After breakfast, a comfortable speedboat awaits you for a short navigation along the coast to enjoy the landscape and wildlife. At the rock formation, put on your snorkel gear and jump in to explore the world under the water via the channel. If you’re lucky, you may see a variety of shark species, reef fish, sea turtles, sting rays, sea lions, and more! Enjoy a lunch on board and then take a stroll along the secluded beach, or enjoy another swim or snorkel in the shallow bay. From here, we’ll return to the port town.

**DAY 3**

Today we hike along a rocky trail to the cliffs for an incredible view, a nesting site for many marine birds. Later, relax on Loberia Beach, or take a swim with the sea lions and sea turtles. In the afternoon, we’ll visit a different beach, Punta Carola, and enjoy the beautiful sunset.

**DAY 4**

Spend your last morning hunting for the perfect souor taking photos before your transfer to San Cristoba airport and returning to the mainland. Overnight in Guayaquil and fly to Lima - Cusco.

**DAY 5**

We pick you up from the airport to take to your hotel in Cusco afterwards we go and visit Qoricancha, Sacsayhuaman, Quenqo, Pucapucara, Tambomachay all these arqueological places are around Cusco city. This tour start from 2:00 pm til 7:00 pm.

**DAY 6**

This day we have to be ready at 8:30 am for our tour Sacred Valley where we visit Pisaq market and arqueological area, we have Buffet lunch then we go to Ollantaytambo to take our train to Aguas Calientes town where we sleep.

**DAY 7**

We wake up to go Machupicchu with 30 minutes journey bus to the ruins where we recieve 2 hours guiding around Machupicchu then you will have free time to explore the ruins for yourself til the time of your train back to Ollantaytambo then by car or bus to Cusco.

**DAY 8**

We visit in this day Moray the circular terraces and salt flat of Maras.

**DAY 9**

We take you to the airport. "BUEN VIAJE!!!!" END OF OUR SERVICES
