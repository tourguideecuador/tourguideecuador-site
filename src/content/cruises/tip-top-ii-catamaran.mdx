The Tip Top II is the perfect vessel for those who want to discover the Galapagos.

The Tip Top II is the perfect vessel for those who want to discover the Galapagos in a comfortable and intimate setting while receiving excellent, personalized service.

Each of the craft's nine cabins comes with a private bathroom (equipped with both hot and cold water), a personal safe, and a wardrobe.

Some of the many luxuries you'll enjoy on the yacht's three decks are wall-to-wall carpeting, air-conditioning, TV/VCR with sound system, an inviting dining room, a well-stocked bar, a library filled with a variety of reading materials, and a spacious sundeck.

With years of operation and a recent overhaul, this catamaran is ready to show you the best of the Galapagos!The Tip Top II offers 6 different itineraries. All itineraries are subject to change without prior notice due to park regulations, weather or other reasons.

Scheduled tour bookings are not confirmed until payment is received ( by Paypal or wire transfer bank*).

In any event, Hidden Secrets strongly advises you to take out travel insurance… its well worth the piece of mind.

We always suggest a call, email or text the day prior to your tour to ensure all your details are as they should be. It is not obligatory, but it helps.

If confirmed standard departure tour bookings are cancelled the following fees apply:

More than 7 days in advance – 10% of original fee.

3-7 days in advance – 50% of tour cost.

Within 72 hours of commencement – refunds will not be available.

If changes are made more than 6 days prior to original booking there is no charge.. the rebooked date must be within 2 months.
