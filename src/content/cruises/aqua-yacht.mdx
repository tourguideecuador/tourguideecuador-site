The days onboard normally begin early to make the most out of your time on the islands. Wake up call around 7 am, unless activities start earlier

**A DAY ABOARD M/Y AQUA:**

Upon returning on board you will either have a snack and have some time to relax; or lunch (depending on the duration of the morning program).

We will often head towards the next site on the itinerary while you are enjoying lunch.

Upon arrival, the afternoon program will begin. Normally this ends before dinner is ready which means some leisure time for you to relax and refresh.

During or after dinner the motor yacht starts moving again to make sure you’ll wake up to a different view to the night before.
