The most beautiful landscapes of the Ecuadorian Andes

Condor Watching + Antisana Reserve (7 – 8 HOURS)

During this full-day tour, you will be able to enjoy the beautiful landscapes of the Antisana reserve, renowned for its condor sightings.

Upon arrival at the reserve, you will mount a horse to ride along the trails of this reserve, enjoying not only gorgeous views of the snow-capped volcano but also admiring the endemic flora and fauna whilst searching for the majestic condor, the national bird of all Andean countries on which many tales are based, as well as the very popular tune of "El Condor Pasa".

We will ride for about 2 hours accompanied by local cowboys (chagras) who know the ins and outs of the trails and the reserve. You will have some more time to enjoy the sights before heading back to your accommodations in Quito.

An active day out in nature, full of fresh air.
