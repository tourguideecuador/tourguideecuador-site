Two hours northwest of Quito you will find an extreme change in climate and vegetation. Driving via a windy road towards Mindo you will ascend from 2800 m. to 1600 m. and enter the cloud forest. 

We will leave the hotel at 8 AM and take you to the fairytale landscapes that comprise the cloud forest. A place for nature and adventure lovers alike you will find a world of things to do and see here. Considered 1 of the 5 most bio-diverse places in the world and especially for those into birding this is a place not to miss!

As it is impossible to see and do everything on this full-day tour we advise you to choose a maximum of 3 from the options below:



- On our way to Mindo town, we will pass by Inti Nana, an interactive museum located right on the Equator where you can participate in experiments only possible on the Equator and where you will receive an explanation of the various indigenous cultures living in Ecuador.

- A hike to the Pahuma Sacred Waterfalls should not be missed if you are a lover of orchids. Here alone you will find over 200 different types of orchids. Located just an hour out of Quito and right on the way to Mindo.

- Alambi is a paradise for bird lovers. Hummingbirds, Toucans, and many more birds can be spotted here. From time to time this is also a good site to see the Andean Spectacled Bear roaming around.

- Chocolate. Who doesn't love chocolate? Ecuador is a producer of world-class chocolate and in Mindo, they have made it their specialty. See what is actually involved in getting those nice treats to your door, from growing, to cultivation, to harvesting, to producing fine products to eat and drink. Something every chocolate lover will appreciate.

- Butterflies, orchids, and hummingbirds. Right outside of Mindo we will take you on a tour to see how orchids, hummingbirds, and butterflies all work together in this pristine environment.

- Hiking and ziplining. In and around Mindo you will find some great hikes of easy to moderate level. We will take you on a hike of around 5.6 km (3.5 miles) in which you will be seeing as much as possible of what actually makes up the cloud forest. After the hike, you will go ziplining, fly high above the treetops from one place to the other with your choice of 3 or 10 lines.
