In Ecuador, we have 2000 volcanoes (including Galapagos, 89 on mainland) most of them actives, and some of them durmients, and the most important part is that is possible to visit some of them with the correctly climatizacion and equipment.

The climatization:
* Day 1 Rucu Pichincha (4780 masl)
* Day 2 Ilinizas (5110 masl)
* Day 3 Overnight Cotopaxi (5897 masl) refuge and climb early in the morning.
