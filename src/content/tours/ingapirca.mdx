Start at 8h00 am. Drive on the Panamerican highway through the dramatic and picturesque Andes mountain and visit the largest Cañari - Incas Complex, with history, archeology, ethnobotany and understand the Incas cosmovision. Heading back to Cuenca stop and see the workshop of native ladies weavers of Panama hat.

Stop for a local snack cascaritas by the road.

Visit a local Catholic impressive church of Biblian constructed in a cliff of these dramatic mountains.
