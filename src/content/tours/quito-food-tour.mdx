We leave the hotel around 09h30 am. Passing first by a colorful vegetable market of Quito, choosing the best ingredients to prepare a local soup. Ecuador counts a total of 227 types of soups. The concept is to share the local gastronomy which has been fussioned along the time with pre-Incan, incan, Spanish and the current tendencies to create the most exquisite flavors of our cuisine. We will interact by preparing our favorite recipes such as a choice of 2 kinds of local hot sauces, and a choice between a shrimp ceviche or a potato cream soup with cheese and avocado. Back in the hotel around 3 pm
