We spent 2 days with Andreas, first to the Mindo Cloud Forest & then to Ottovalo Market & Cotocachi. He was a fantastic guide & was also fun to spend a full day with. He is so knowledgeable & was eager to share both the history & culture of his beautiful country. He was also flexible & will adjust the days activities to fit your preferences. It would be hard to find a better guide, we started to feel like family & will miss him.
